What we do

The Thrunton Trails Association is a Charity set up to help sustain and manage the trail network across at Thrunton woods

Welcome to the Thrunton Trail Association

The Thrunton Trail Association is a community-driven organization dedicated to promoting and preserving the mountain bike trails in and around Thrunton, North England. Our mission is to create a network of world-class, high-adrenaline trails that showcase the rugged beauty of our region while providing an exhilarating riding experience for intermediate to extreme enduro enthusiasts.

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate local riders, the Thrunton Trail Association was established to address the growing demand for challenging and technical trails in the North East of England. With its rugged landscape, steep valleys, and rocky outcrops, Thrunton offers a perfect setting for building trails that push riders to their limits. Our association brings together local riders, landowners, and trail enthusiasts to develop and maintain trails that cater to the most adventurous and skilled riders.

Our Trails

The Thrunton Trail Association is responsible for developing and maintaining over 20 miles of purpose-built trails in the Thrunton area. Our trails are designed for intermediate to extreme enduro riders who crave technical singletrack, rocky drop-offs, and challenging climbs. Whether you're looking for a thrilling descent or a technically demanding climb, our trails will test your skills and push you to new limits.

Trail Difficulty

  • Intermediate: 30% of our trails are designed for intermediate riders who enjoy flowing singletrack, rollers, and small jumps.
  • Advanced: 40% of our trails are designed for advanced riders who crave more challenging terrain, including rocky drop-offs, tight switchbacks, and technical climbs.
  • Extreme: 30% of our trails are designed for extreme enduro enthusiasts who seek out the toughest terrain, including steep chutes, rocky ledges, and treacherous descents.
